Pre-Christmas Activities

Celebrating the holiday season was a heartwarming experience filled with joy and cheer!

As Christmas approaches, the atmosphere at school becomes a little lighter, with everyone eagerly counting down the days until the holiday break. Between finishing assignments, studying for exams, and wrapping up projects, it can feel like a hectic time, but there's still a sense of excitement in the air. Classrooms are filled with festive decorations, and many teachers bring in treats or plan holiday-themed activities to keep spirits high. Despite the workload, it’s hard not to get caught up in the cheerful anticipation of the season, whether it's the thought of holiday traditions, gift exchanges, or simply a break from school. In the end, it's a mix of stress and joy, and everyone is ready for a little rest and time with family.

Project Journey

At the start of the project, we were full of excitement and had countless ideas for the website.
We were eager to dive in, so we spent a lot of time watching website animations for inspiration. However, only a handful of those ideas made it to the final product.
The biggest challenge we faced was balancing the overwhelming number of ideas with the uncertainty of where to begin.
Despite the excitement, it was difficult to focus and prioritize, which made the process of starting feel both overwhelming and exciting at the same time
